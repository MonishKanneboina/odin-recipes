Quick and Easy Brownies

Description

This easy brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish for a chocolaty treat!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: Collect all ingredients and ensure they are edible.
  2. Preheat: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  3. Spread: Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Decorate: Decorate with walnut halves.
  5. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy: Let cool on a wire rack before cutting into squares.
