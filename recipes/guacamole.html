Easy Guacamole

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: Collect all ingredients and ensure they are edible.
  2. Mash: Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  3. Stir: Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic.
  4. Season: Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Enjoy: Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
